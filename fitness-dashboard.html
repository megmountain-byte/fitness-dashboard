Full Body — Push emphasis · Chest · Shoulders · Legs · Back · Core
Matrix Treadmill
Cardio warmup — every session
15 min ~0.62 mi · moderate pace
How to do this

Set speed to a comfortable walk (around 3.0–3.5 mph). Step on once it's moving, hold the side rails until you find your footing. Keep an easy pace for 15 minutes to warm up — not a cardio workout, just get the blood flowing.

Leg Press
Quads, glutes, hamstrings
3 sets 12 reps ~75 lbs
How to do this

Sit back with your back flat against the pad. Place feet shoulder-width apart on the platform. Push until your legs are almost straight — don't lock your knees — then slowly bend back to about 90°. Feet higher on the platform hits glutes more; lower hits quads more.

Leg Extension
Quads — front of thigh
3 sets 12 reps ~35 lbs
How to do this

Sit with your back against the pad and ankles hooked behind the lower roller. Extend your legs up until they're almost straight, squeezing your quads at the top. Lower slowly — don't let the weight drop. Keep the movement smooth and controlled throughout.

Chest Press
Chest, front shoulders, triceps
3 sets 12 reps ~45 lbs
How to do this

Sit tall, back flat against the pad. Grip both handles at chest height, elbows slightly below shoulder level. Push straight out until arms are nearly extended, then bring back slowly. Don't let your lower back arch away from the pad.

Lat Pulldown
Lats, upper back — pulling down
3 sets 12 reps ~65 lbs
How to do this

Sit and secure your thighs under the pad. Grip the bar slightly wider than shoulder-width. Pull the bar down toward your upper chest, driving your elbows down and back — think "elbows to your back pockets." Hold briefly at the bottom, then slowly let the bar rise back up. Keep your torso upright; don't lean way back.

Shoulder Press
Shoulders — overhead press
3 sets 12 reps ~45 lbs
How to do this

Sit upright, back against the pad, feet flat on the floor. Grip handles at shoulder height, palms facing forward. Press straight up until arms are nearly extended, then lower slowly back to shoulder level. Keep your core tight — don't let your lower back arch.

Abdominal Crunch
Core — abs
3 sets 12 reps ~65 lbs
How to do this

Sit with your back against the pad, feet on the footrests. Grip the handles and curl your upper body forward in a crunching motion, contracting your abs at the bottom. Don't pull with your arms — the work should come from your core. Slowly return to the upright position.

Back Extension
Lower back, glutes
3 sets 12 reps ~65 lbs
How to do this

Sit in the machine with the pad against your lower back. Cross your arms over your chest or hold the side handles. Lean back, extending your torso against the resistance, then slowly return forward. Don't hyperextend — stop when your back is straight. Control the return; don't let the weight pull you forward fast.

Biceps Curl
Biceps — front of arm
3 sets 12 reps ~20 lbs
How to do this

Sit with your upper arms resting on the pad, palms facing up. Curl the handles up toward your shoulders, squeezing your biceps at the top. Lower slowly — don't let the weight drop. Keep your upper arms still on the pad throughout; only your forearms move.

Seated Leg Curl
Hamstrings — back of thigh
3 sets 12 reps ~35 lbs
How to do this

Sit with the lower roller pad on top of your ankles and the back pad behind your knees (adjust so your knees line up with the machine's pivot point). Curl your heels down and back underneath the seat. Squeeze briefly, then return slowly. Align knees with orange pivot point as the label says.

Seated Row
Mid back, lats — horizontal pull
3 sets 12 reps ~55 lbs
How to do this

Adjust the chest pad so it's comfortable. Grip the handles and sit upright. Pull toward your lower chest/stomach, squeezing your shoulder blades together at the end. Slowly extend back out. Keep your chest against the pad and torso upright throughout — don't lean or rock.

Shoulder Press
Shoulders — overhead press
3 sets 12 reps ~45 lbs
How to do this

Sit upright, back against the pad. Press up from shoulder height until arms are nearly extended, then lower slowly. Keep your core tight throughout.

Rotary Torso
Obliques, core — rotational
3 sets 12 reps each side ~45 lbs
How to do this

Sit and grip the handles at chest height. Rotate your torso to one side against the resistance, then slowly return to center. Complete all reps on one side before switching. Keep your hips still — only your upper body rotates. Set up the machine so the range of motion feels comfortable, not forced.

Triceps Extension
Triceps — back of arm
3 sets 12 reps ~45 lbs
How to do this

Sit with the pad adjusted so your elbows line up with the machine's pivot. Grip the handles with palms facing down. Extend your arms downward until they're straight, squeezing your triceps. Slowly let them return up. Keep your elbows on the pad and your upper arms still — only forearms move.

Dependent Curl
Biceps, back — bilateral curl
3 sets 12 reps ~65 lbs
How to do this

Sit at the machine and grip both handles. Curl upward, bringing the handles toward your shoulders — both arms move together. Keep your upper arms still and your back against the pad. Lower slowly. The heavier weight compared to the isolated bicep curl is normal since both arms work simultaneously.
